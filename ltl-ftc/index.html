Long time listener, first time caller is a book being printed signature by signature, weekly from February 1st until April 21st, 2024.

Long time listener, first time caller’s content is free-associative and improvised, each signature takes a previous project as its point of departure. The book encourages participation from readers through the form of comments and submitted content. It favors messy conversations and questions over neat conclusions.

Everything about the book can change next week.


  1. Announcing   23 27 February 2024  
  2. Across time and space   15 February 2024  
  3. No fortune  1 February 2024  
Long time listener, first time caller is designed and edited by Julio Correa Estrada. It is a thesis book being completed in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Master of Fine Arts at Yale University with a major in Graphic Design. It is distributed physically at Green Hall (1156 Chapel St, New Haven, CT) and at www.two-nouns.com/ltl-ftc
