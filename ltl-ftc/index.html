Long time listener, First time caller is being printed signature by signature, weekly from February 1st until April 21st, culminating in a “thesis book”.

Long time listener, First time caller’s content is free-associative and improvised, each signature takes a previous project as its point of departure. The book encourages participation from readers through the form of comments and submitted content. It favors messy conversations and questions over neat conclusions.

Everything about the book can change next week.

  2. No fortune  1 February 2024
